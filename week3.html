Miskito Burger

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1/4 lb ground pork
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix ground beef and pork together in a bowl.
  2. Add all spices, Worcestershire sauce, onions, and cilantro.
  3. Form the mixture into two burger patties.
  4. Heat a pan or grill to medium-high and cook the patties for about 4 minutes per side.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns lightly.
  6. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, and cheese as desired.
  7. Add some french fries to the side.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
